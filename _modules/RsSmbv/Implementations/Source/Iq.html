		Enables/disables the I/Q modulation. Note: Interdependencies
		The following functions cannot be activated simultaneously. They deactivate each other. \n
			- The internal baseband generator ([:SOURce<hw>]:BB:<DigStd>:STATe) and the external digital baseband input (method ``RsSmbv.source.bbin.state()`` )

		:return: state: 1 | ON | 0| OFF
		"""
